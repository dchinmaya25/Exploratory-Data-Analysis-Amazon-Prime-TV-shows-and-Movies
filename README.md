The exploratory data analysis of Amazon Prime Video’s catalog provides a comprehensive understanding of its content distribution, audience preferences, and performance trends. The analysis revealed that drama, comedy, and action are the most dominant genres, indicating audience preference for emotionally engaging and entertaining stories. However, niche categories such as documentaries and animation also showed higher average IMDb ratings, suggesting potential growth opportunities in those segments.Overall, the insights emphasize the importance of data-driven decision-making in shaping Amazon Prime’s content strategy. By aligning content production and acquisition with audience trends, Prime Video can enhance user engagement, satisfaction, and retention, ultimately achieving sustainable business growth in the competitive streaming landscape.
